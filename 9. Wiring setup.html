This project was a great test of wiring, soldering, and ee skills. It involved determining how to safely setup industrial LEDs that the client could easily move between locations. It had to be safe (AC current concerns), mobile, and plug and play for multiple LED outputs. Finally, heat management was a consern that had to be addressed and resolved to protect all components in the system from damage.
